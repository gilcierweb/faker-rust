# faker::quotes::shakespeare

Generate Shakespeare quotes.

```rust
use faker::quotes::shakespeare;

shakespeare::hamlet_quote() //=> "To be, or not to be: that is the question."

shakespeare::as_you_like_it_quote() //=> "Can one desire too much of a good thing?."

shakespeare::king_richard_iii_quote() //=> "Now is the winter of our discontent."

shakespeare::romeo_and_juliet_quote() //=> "O Romeo, Romeo! wherefore art thou Romeo?."
```
